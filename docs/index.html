About me

Welcome to Manon's World of Virtual Reality and Beyond!

Manon Vessiot || Resume


I'm Manon, a computer engineer specializing in VR and AR. Working at the University of Aix-Marseille in the virtual reality center of the Mediterranean (CRVM), I create captivating experiences across web, mobile, desktop, and headsets. From immersive landscapes to lifelike avatars, I push the boundaries of technology to transport users into extraordinary digital realms.

Photography and videography are my passions, capturing the beauty of the world and telling compelling stories. When not immersed in pixels, I embark on exhilarating hiking adventures, finding inspiration in nature's serenity. Traveling broadens my horizons, infusing authenticity into my work.

Sports like parachuting and paragliding provide an adrenaline rush, reminding me of the thrill of breaking boundaries. Video games and movies fuel my imagination, influencing my creative endeavors.

Join me as we explore the boundless possibilities of virtual reality, celebrate visual arts, and embark on thrilling adventures.
