OLD THINGS

Let's dig into the past, 2015 - 2018

I have a fondness for tinkering, especially when it comes to working with paper. It all began with origami, and then I delved into the art of kirigami. As my journey progressed, I stumbled upon the captivating realm of 3D origami, which I eventually adapted to suit my own style. However, my creative endeavors don't stop there. I find joy in repurposing items like old beer capsules or anything that happens to be lying around. I've even experimented with the possibilities offered by a 3D pen.
