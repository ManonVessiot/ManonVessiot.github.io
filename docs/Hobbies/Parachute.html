FIRST JUMP

UPnFLY Avignon, April 27, 2023

At long last, the moment arrived. After years of envisioning and longing for it, I finally summoned the courage to take the leap! Though I had been assured of this opportunity years ago, as time passed with no signs of progress, I decided to seize control and propel myself into the unknown.

The experience was swift yet profound, leaving an indelible mark on my being. I am resolute in my intention to embark on this adventure once more, and next time, I aspire to undertake it entirely on my own!
