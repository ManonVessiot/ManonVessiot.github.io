AUDIO VISUAL

Let me show you the world through my lens!

Welcome to my visual adventures! Join me as I capture the world through images and videos, bringing moments to life. I'll share breathtaking visuals that transport you to different places and evoke emotions. From stunning photos to immersive videos, each piece tells a unique story. Together, let's explore the power of visual storytelling and embrace the wonders of the world. Welcome to my world of audio-visual magic!


Here are some of my recent work.

SUMMER 2022

First outing of the S5, July, 2022

Here marks the beginning of my photography journey. It has been a long-standing desire of mine, but due to not knowing where to commence, I consistently postponed it. However, today, with the need to enhance my work, receiving guidance from my colleagues, and having access to equipment, I feel that the time has come. Therefore, I utilized my vacation time to engage in practice. Among the various aspects, I find post-production to be the most challenging, but I derive enjoyment from creating multiple versions and experimenting with it.

OLD MEMORIES

Let's dive into the past, 2014 - 2018 (I think)

Photography has always held a special place in my heart, yet I have constantly struggled with knowing where to begin and how to process the captured images. Determined to overcome this hurdle, I made the decision to delve into post-production by revitalizing my old photos.

Engaging in this practice not only allows me to improve my skills but also evokes a wave of nostalgia as it rekindles cherished memories from the past.
