PARAGLIDING

Let's go fly!

Welcome to the exhilarating world of paragliding! Join me as I soar through the skies, capturing the freedom and thrill of this incredible sport. Let's chase the adrenaline, witness breathtaking views, and experience the joy of defying gravity. Welcome to my world of airborne adventure!


Here are some of my recent flights.

FIRST SOLO FLIGHT

Takeoff from Cuges-les-Pins, January 21, 2024

That's it ! I made my first big paragliding flight. Guided by radio I took off, piloted and landed. In the space of a day and a half of training I was able to fly alone. I thought that was the goal, the end I wanted to achieve... I was completely wrong. Only 2 days later, I was back with 3 more flights. In fact, it was only the beginning!

FIRST FLIGHT

Chamonix seen from above, February 26, 2022

Technically, this isn't my inaugural flight. However, any previous experiences pale in comparison to the one I just had! The view from up there was absolutely breathtaking. Not only did I get the chance to pilot the aircraft, but I also indulged in exhilarating aerobatic maneuvers. The rush of adrenaline during the flight and the subsequent surge of excitement were two moments that I yearn to experience repeatedly. Even though I had a moment of queasiness afterward, feeling as if my insides were about to make an unwelcome appearance, I still crave that sensation!
